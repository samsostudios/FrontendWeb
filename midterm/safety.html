Rules of the road

On roads ride with the flow of traffic, not against it

Obey all traffic laws

Communicate your intention to turn, stop, and change lanes using hand signals.

Equipment

Wear a properly fitted bike helmet

Make sure your bike is properly adjusted and maintained

Inflate your tires to the recommended pressure

Be visible

Wear bright clothing during the day

Wear reflective clothing at night

Front and rear lights are recommended
